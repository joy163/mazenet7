About Bibliothèque

Curated excellence since MMXXIV

Founded MMXXIV

A Passion for the
Written Word

Bibliothèque was born from a singular conviction: that books deserve to be presented with the same reverence as great art. We believe the act of acquiring a book — the weight of it in your hands, the promise of the pages — is a ritual that deserves to be honoured.

Our founders, passionate bibliophiles with decades of experience in publishing and luxury retail, set out to create a bookstore that would serve not just readers, but collectors. A place where every volume is hand-selected, every order is thoughtfully packaged, and every customer is treated as a patron of the literary arts.

Today, Bibliothèque serves customers in over 40 countries, and our commitment to curation, craft, and service remains unchanged from the day we first opened our doors.
