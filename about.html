About Us

Curated excellence since MMXXIV

Founded MMXXIV

A Passion for the
Written Word

Bibliothèque was born from a singular conviction: that books deserve to be presented with the same reverence as great art. We believe the act of acquiring a book is a ritual that deserves to be honoured.

Our founders — passionate bibliophiles with decades in publishing and luxury retail — set out to create a bookstore that would serve not just readers, but collectors. A place where every volume is hand-selected and every customer treated as a patron of the literary arts.

Today we serve customers in 40+ countries with our commitment to curation, craft, and service unchanged from day one.
